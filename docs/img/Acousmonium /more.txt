In 2018 and 2020, I was invited to participate as a composer and performer in Acousmatic music festival. With the mentorship of great composers of France like Fransua Belle; legendary author "China for us" Jean-Claude Eloy; professor at Antona Bruknera University, Daniel Terudji - senior director of GRM in Paris and Austria
Considered a pioneering unit in electroacoustic, acousmatic and concrete music, the GRM (Groupe de Recherche Musicale) has been a unique laboratory for sound experiments since 1958.
Integrated into the INA (Institut National de l'Audiovisuel) since 1975, the GRM has maintained a strong involvement in the field of experimental music and continues to expand its repertoire every year.

Today, the INA GRM welcomes internationally renowned composers in residence, produces concerts in France and abroad (Festival PRÉSENCES électronique, Multiphonies concert season), and develops internationally recognized music production and sound creation software.
It transmits to a wide audience a constantly evolving cultural heritage through radio broadcasts on France Musique (L'Expérimentale), discographic production (monographic CD boxes, Re-collection GRM), publications (Portraits polychromes, SPECTRES), and educational content (Creamus, Éduthèque).
