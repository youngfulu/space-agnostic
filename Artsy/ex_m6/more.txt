

> Commissioned by ENSA Bourges and Galerie La Box.
This project was presented as an art book and a single-channel video installation. A significant contribution was made by Kristina Vodorezova, PhD candidate at the Academic Medical Center, University of Amsterdam, who provided expert insight drawn from her current research on neuronal transmission and the formation of memory- related neural clusters.
This project focuses on transferring the viewer to an outer-world reality of distorted memories through video art with a blurred narration of a young girl character being lost in her memories during a trip to an unknown Middle Eastern country, and a book as her diary notes.
