In 2023, I was invited to participate in the media art exhibition held by the 36 degree foundation in Paris. I presented media art and live audiovisual performances.


Psych.e / 36 degrés and the Charlot Gallery imagine a new exhibition inspired by mystical themes, spiritualism and various realities. In a unique space in front of the Grand Palais, we will provoke the meeting with the public, technology, science and spirituality through many works, performances and workshops. Psyc.he offers a contrasting environment, housed in two former Haussmannian apartments in Paris, where the very cause of our contemporary disconnection - technology - serves as a gateway to a universe of new discoveries and spiritual awakening. Creating a new space for self-discovery and the exploration of awakened practices, Psych.e is a flexible space giving life to workshops and performances, breaking the standards of scenography, programming and behavior of the white-cube.
